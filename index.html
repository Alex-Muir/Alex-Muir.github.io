An Ode To Coffee by Alex Muir

I find coffee very interesting. Coffee has been a ritual for me since I was a child. I used to make cappuccinos for my dad and uncles on an Olympia Cremina that my dad purchased before I was born (He still has it, It still works). Learning about extraction during brewing, chemical reactions during roasting, and green coffee processing techniques helps push aside the veil of mystery surrounding coffee but, honestly it still feels like magic.
